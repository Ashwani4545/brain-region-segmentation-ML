Capabilities
End-to-end neuroimaging pipeline

Hypodense detection

Identifies areas darker than surrounding parenchyma — ischemic stroke, oedema, and infarction — using HU thresholding or trained U-Net.

Segmentation mask

Binary lesion map showing exact hypodense regions overlaid on the original scan with semi-transparent red fill and orange contour outlines.

Explainable AI summary

Plain-language clinical interpretation of findings, lesion load percentage, and a colour-coded confidence badge for rapid assessment.

Radiology-style report

Auto-generated text reports via report_generator.py with lesion load metrics and volumetric analysis.

Multi-format support

Accepts DICOM (.dcm) with Hounsfield unit metadata for maximum accuracy, or standard JPG/PNG using adaptive intensity thresholding.

Export overlay

Download the AI-annotated overlay directly. UUID-prefixed filenames prevent collision during concurrent uploads in the Django backend.

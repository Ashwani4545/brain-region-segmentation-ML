About NeuroDetect AI®

A state-of-the-art clinical intelligence platform designed to automatically detect, segment, and highlight hypodense regions in Brain NCCT scans.

Our Technology Core

At the core of NeuroDetect AI is a sophisticated dual-mode physics-based and deep-learning pipeline designed for maximum accuracy and clinical relevance.

2.5D U-Net Architecture

Utilizes multi-slice contextual information for robust 3D-like segmentation while remaining highly computationally efficient for web deployment.

HU Thresholding

For raw DICOM files, we apply exact physics-based clinical windows (WL=35, WW=100) to ensure pinpoint density accuracy.

Adaptive Modeling

For standard JPG/PNG formats lacking metadata, we use statistical outlier detection to reliably highlight hypodense anomalies.

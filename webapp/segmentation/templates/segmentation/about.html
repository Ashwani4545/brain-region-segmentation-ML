About NeuroDetect AI

NeuroDetect AI is a state-of-the-art web application designed to automatically detect, segment, and highlight hypodense regions (such as those caused by ischemic stroke, oedema, or infarction) in Brain Non-Contrast Computed Tomography (NCCT) scans.

Our Technology

At the core of NeuroDetect AI is a dual-mode physics-based and deep-learning pipeline:

  • 2.5D U-Net Architecture: Utilizes multi-slice contextual information for robust 3D-like segmentation while remaining computationally efficient.
  • Hounsfield Unit Thresholding: For raw DICOM files, we apply exact physics-based clinical windows (WL=35, WW=100) for pinpoint accuracy.
  • Adaptive Intensity Modeling: For standard JPG/PNG formats lacking metadata, we use statistical outlier detection to reliably highlight hypodense anomalies.

Purpose & Disclaimer

This application was developed by Ashwani Pandey as an academic and research project. It is not a certified medical device and should never be used to make clinical or diagnostic decisions without the review of a qualified medical professional.
